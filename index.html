Pavel Zhukov is a seasoned Senior Backend Engineer with over 15 years of experience in backend development, system architecture, API design, primarily using Ruby on Rails. He has worked with companies like Toptal, ClickClickDrive, and several fintech and government-related organizations. Pavel is skilled in a wide range of technologies including PostgreSQL, Redis, Docker, Elasticsearch, and GraphQL, and has hands-on experience with microservices, performance optimization, and mentoring. He holds a degree in Technical Cybernetics and Informatics, and has completed certifications in "Oracle PL/SQL" and "Software as a Service" from UC Berkeley. Known for being self-motivated and technically versatile, he is passionate about scalable backend systems and continuous improvement.
